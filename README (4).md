# MoodBoard — Studio Digital Fashion Stylist 🎨✨

**MoodBoard** is an interactive personal stylist studio that translates your unique moods, feelings, and aesthetic visions into beautifully coordinate fashion lookbooks, hair recommendations, makeup guides, and instant virtual tries-on.

---

## ✦ Core Features

### 1. **Vibe & Mood Interpreter**
Describe how you feel, where you're going, or the exact aesthetic density you want (e.g., *“Rainy Sunday morning coffee crawl”* or *“Neon-lit midnight city explore”*), and the assistant constructs:
- **Cohesive Color Palettes**: Stylized color mapping matching the key tone.
- **Accented Accessories**: Jewelry, layers, bags, and sunglasses.
- **Hair & Makeup Coordinates**: Targeted styling patterns and complete skincare/makeup base definitions.
- **Shoes & Heels Recommendations**: Perfect height and style constraints.

### 2. **Interactive Design Guides**
Step-by-step beauty coordinates listing colors, products, and tailored styling tips for nails, hairstyles, and ambient fragrances.

### 3. **Virtual Try-on Studio**
Upload a portrait, detected via face geometry, and see how the customized outfits fit on you instantly using our responsive try-on simulation canvas.

---

## 🛠️ Stack & Architecture

- **Frontend**: React 19, Vite, Tailwind CSS (V4), Motion animations
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Typography**: Inter (UI Core) & Space Grotesk (Display Headings)
- **Backend & Proxy API**: Express & Node.js
